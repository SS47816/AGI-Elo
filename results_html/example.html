DriveELO Interactive Visualizations

About These Visualizations

These interactive visualizations display the rating distributions and performance metrics from the DriveELO system. You can interact with the plots to explore the data in detail.

Features:

  • Hover over elements to see detailed information
  • Click on legend items to hide/show data series
  • Use the toolbar to pan, zoom, and save the visualization

Rating Distributions

This visualization shows the distribution of test case ratings and model ratings.

Outcome vs Rating

This visualization shows the relationship between rating differences and outcomes.
